Major Project

### 1. *Ticket Sales Forecasting 
***Inputs*: Team performance, opponent, weather, day of the week, historical ticket sales. 
**ML Models*: Random Forest, XGBoost, LSTM (for time series). 
**Outcome*: Optimize pricing, marketing campaigns, and staffing.

🧠 Example Use Cases After Feature Addition
📈 Pricing Optimization : Simulate attendance if ticket_price_avg is increased by 10% and marketing is doubled.
📣 Marketing Allocation : Predict impact of extra $5000 marketing spend for low-demand matches.
🧍‍♂️ Staffing Forecast : Predict needed security/staff based on expected attendance ± buffer.

Columns in the dataset :
1️⃣ dynamic_pricing_flag
Meaning: Did the match use dynamic pricing (ticket prices change with demand, like surge pricing)?
Values:
0 → No dynamic pricing (fixed ticket price for all)
1 → Yes, dynamic pricing was applied (prices adjusted before/during sales)
👉 Useful for understanding whether flexible pricing affects ticket sales.

2️⃣ holiday_flag
Meaning: Did the match fall on a holiday or weekend?
Values:
0 → Regular working day (Mon–Fri)
1 → Holiday or weekend (Saturday/Sunday in generated dataset, could also include public holidays if you have them)
👉 Helps capture the effect of holidays on attendance and demand.

3️⃣ promotion_flag
Meaning: Was there a promotion/offer/discount campaign tied to ticket sales?
Values:
0 → No promotion (normal sales)
1 → Promotion/discount/freebies offered
👉 Helps measure marketing impact on attendance.

4️⃣ special_event_flag
Meaning: Was this match a special event (final, semi-final, big rivalry, celebrity appearance, etc.)?
Values:
0 → Regular match
1 → Special/high-profile event
👉 Useful for staffing optimization (finals and rivalry matches often need more security/staff).

### 2. Merchandise Sales Prediction 
* Inputs: Player stats, recent wins/losses, ad campaigns, social sentiment. 
* ML Models: Regression models, time series models. 
* Outcome: Inventory and logistics optimization.

performance_index ---> A numerical score indicating recent team performance or player form, typically between 0 and 1. ---> 0.75

team_wins_last_5 ---> Number of wins the team had in the last 5 matches. ---> 3

ad_campaign_spend ---> Amount of money spent on advertising campaigns recently (could be daily, weekly, etc.) ---> 10000.0

sentiment_score	---> Score representing public sentiment or fan sentiment about the team or event (range: -1 to 1) ---> 0.2

historical_avg_merch_sales ---> Average merchandise sales historically for similar events or teams. ---> 1500.0

merch_sales_lag_1 ---> Merchandise sales in the immediately previous event or time period. ---> 1450.0

merch_sales_lag_2 ---> Merchandise sales in the event before the previous one (two events ago). ---> 1600.0

merch_sales_roll_3 ---> Rolling average merchandise sales over the last 3 events. ---> 1520.0

days_since_last_match ---> Number of days since the last match was played. ---> 7

month ---> Numeric month when the match/event is happening (1 = January, ..., 12 = December) ---> 10

is_weekend ---> Binary indicator if the event is on a weekend (1 = Saturday or Sunday, 0 = Weekday) ---> 0

team ---> Categorical variable representing the team playing or hosting the event ---> "TeamA"

day_of_week ---> Numeric day of the week (0 = Monday, ..., 6 = Sunday) ---> 0 (Monday)
